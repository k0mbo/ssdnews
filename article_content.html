JUBA – South Sudan’s main armed opposition group on Tuesday evening issued a damning statement denying rumors circulating in the Sudanese capital Khartoum alleging defection of the group’s military chief of staff General Simon Gatwech Dual.

Since last week, speculations have it that Dual has been allegedly planning to declare end of the revitalized peace agreement with some opposition officials claiming to be in direct communication – in Khartoum – with the powerful opposition commander.

In the statement signed by the group’s military spokesman William Gatjiath Deng, the opposition command says it there is no truth behind the claims and that senior opposition officials close to Machar including the group’s military intelligence chief General Dhiling Keah were behind the speculations.
